Robin Goods 👽

Help our little Alien friend get the gems and escape the shopkeeper!

Storyline

Bob the Alien just watched Robin Hood!🦊🏹 Now he's up to no good sneaking into shop and taking the MOST prized possessions!💰 Will he escape... or is the AI shopkeeper too cunning? Play and find out! 🎮

Development Process

The game used Blender art and many Unity assets to create the shop. Challenges included coding the AI bot - there were many intracacies that I had to tackle to make it run smoothly without being too hard for the player. I was able to acheive the right balance using Markov chain and A* pathfinding in the maze.

Technologies Used

  • Unity Engine for game mechanics and physics.
  • Mixamo for animations.
  • Photoshop used for game cover art.
  • C# for scripting.

Reflections

Building Robin Goods was a great opportunity to apply what I had learned making Randy's escape, while also implementing many new features I decided to tackle. From random maze generation to an AI bot to invisibility potions, this was an incredibly fun game to create with lots of amazing features.
