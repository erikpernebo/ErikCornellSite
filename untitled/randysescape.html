Randy's Escape

Help Randy find the notes and escape the dystopia!

Storyline

Randy was driving home at night when he suddenly drove over black ice and crashed!🚗💥😱 He woke up in a new world with his car in flames!🔥 Now he must survive the zombies and collect the notes scattered across the map to figure out the key to escaping.🔑

Development Process

The game used Blender art and Unity terrain features and assets. Challenges included slow runtime due to sheer map size. I tackled the issue with smart asset management and highly efficient scripts.

Technologies Used

  • Unity Engine for game mechanics, physics, and terrain.
  • Mixamo for animations.
  • Photoshop used for game cover art.
  • C# for scripting.

Reflections

Creating Randy's Escape was a journey that taught me much of my game development skills. Through tackling tough issues with the game speed while still maintaining good story and visuals, I learned to blend storytelling with interactive gameplay to create an exciting game experience.
